# Design Decisions — Final (User-Approved 2026-07-15)

## 1. Artifact Forge — Multi-Block Structure
NOT one block, NOT six blocks. ONE core block with ATTACHMENTS:
- Artifact Forge (core block, central furnace)
- Forge Anvil (attachment)
- Soul Pedestal (attachment)
- Blood Basin (attachment)
- Restriction Table (attachment)
- Refinement Kiln (attachment)
- Dao Engraving Platform (attachment)
GUI changes depending on which attachment you're using.
Player slowly builds a higher tier forge.

## 2. Recipes — Three-Tier System
- **Canon Recipes**: Known throughout cultivation world. Exact ingredients, always works. NPCs know these. Manuals teach these. Sect libraries teach these.
- **Derived Recipes**: Player knows the theory. Can substitute similar herbs. Produces slightly different pills.
- **Experimental Alchemy**: No recipe. Player combines properties. Simulation computes what happens. Explosions, poison, mutation, or entirely new pill.

## 3. Learning — Knowledge Engine
Knowledge is a central progression system. You don't unlock recipes — you acquire knowledge.
Sources: jade slips, manuals, sect libraries, observing masters, experimentation, divine sense, dreams, memories, Wang Lin, ruins, ancient inheritances.
Knowledge unlocks: recipes, techniques, restrictions, formations, theories, diagnoses.

## 4. Craftable vs Found — Technology Tiers
- **Mortal**: Can craft. Clay furnace, iron forge, simple alchemy.
- **Cultivation**: Can craft after understanding. Need spirit materials, arrays, Qi.
- **Ancient**: Cannot simply craft. Need inheritance, blueprint, comprehension, Dao. Some things cannot be reverse engineered.

## 5. Fire Grade — Five Dimensions
Fire has: Temperature, Purity, Stability, Element, Controller.
Pills care about fire PROFILE, not just temperature.
- Mortal charcoal: high temp, low purity, unstable, neutral, uncontrolled
- Spirit flame: high temp, very pure, stable, fire element, controlled
- Nascent Soul flame: temperature irrelevant, perfect control, Dao infused, extremely stable

## 6. Deterministic — Canon Only
Canon recipes: deterministic result, variable quality.
Quality depends on: ingredients, fire, skill, cultivation, environment, luck, tribulation, Dao.

## 7. Cultivation Requirements — Capability-Based, NOT Realm-Gated
Instead of "Foundation Establishment required," use:
Qi, Divine Sense, Soul Strength, Blood, Will, Dao, Body, Knowledge.
A mortal can hammer iron. A mortal cannot refine a soul artifact — because they lack soul strength, not because of an arbitrary realm check.

## 8. Restriction Flag — Visual + Mechanical Progression
As restrictions accumulate: runes appear, flag grows, wind particles, spatial distortion, restrictions orbit, soul screams, lightning, ancient script, reality warps nearby. NPCs recognize it: "That... that's a Restriction Flag..."

## 9. Universal Material System (HIGHEST PRIORITY)
Every material has:
- **Physical**: Density, Hardness, Brittleness, Elasticity
- **Spiritual**: Qi Conductivity, Soul Conductivity, Divine Sense Conductivity, Restriction Affinity, Blood Affinity, Dao Resonance
- **Elemental**: Fire, Water, Earth, Metal, Wood, Lightning, Wind, Yin, Yang, Death, Life, Karma, Time, Space, Dream
- **Historical**: Origin, Age, Previous Owners, Refinements, Tribulations
ALL crafting stations read the SAME material data.

## 10. Three Foundational Systems (BUILD ORDER)
1. Universal Material System
2. Universal Knowledge System
3. Universal Workstation Framework
Then the remaining 12 workstations become fast and consistent.
