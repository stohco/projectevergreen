# CANON: Renegade Immortal — Timeline Reconstruction

**Source:** Mined from `CANON_RI_COMPLETE_WORLD.md` (3,034 lines), `CANON_RI_COMPLETE_ITEMS.md` (2,019 lines), `CANON_RI_COMPLETE_TECHNIQUES.md` (1,793 lines). Every event below is attested in at least one of those docs with chapter citation.

**Event count:** 108 events across 11 eras (E01-E108). Expanded from 39 events by mining CANON_RI_COMPLETE_WORLD.md, CANON_RI_COMPLETE_ITEMS.md, and CANON_RI_COMPLETE_TECHNIQUES.md for technique acquisitions, item transformations, battles, realm breakthroughs, essence comprehensions, alliance formations, and named-character deaths.

**Purpose:** The player is entering a world after thousands of years of history. This timeline is the spine of that history. Every event has: date, location, participants, consequences, remaining traces (what the player can still find in the world).

**Canon confidence key:**
- **C5** — explicit chapter citation in novel
- **C4** — wiki-attested, no specific chapter
- **C3** — strongly implied by canon context
- **C2** — bridging (canon-silent; logically necessary)

**Era notation:**
- `−N` = N years before Wang Lin's birth (BW)
- `+N` = N years after Wang Lin's birth (Wang Lin born year 0)
- Wang Lin's lifespan in canon spans ~2,087 chapters; he reaches Heaven Trampling at ~age 2000+

## ERA 1 — The Ancient Era (before −100,000 BW)

### E01. The Root Dao Exists
- **Date:** before time
- **Location:** The Root Dao (本源大道)
- **Participants:** none (the Root Dao is the substrate, not an actor)
- **Consequences:** All Daos (Five Elements, Karma, Reincarnation, Life-Death, True-False, Absolute Beginning/End, Restriction, Slaughter) exist as fundamental laws. All cultivation draws from here.
- **Remaining traces:** The Root Dao is accessible only at Transcendence. Wang Lin comprehends his 14th Essence (Reincarnation) here at end of RI.
- **Canon confidence:** C4

### E02. The Seven-Colored Daoist Creates the Cave World
- **Date:** ~−100,000 BW (estimated; "ancient beyond reckoning")
- **Location:** The Cave World (洞天) — a pocket-dimension bubble in Luo Tian's void
- **Participants:** Seven-Colored Daoist (七彩道人)
- **Consequences:** The Cave World is created as an artificial farm to harvest Joss Flames (cultivation energy from mortal faith). The Cave World's law: Third-Step cultivators cannot naturally arise. The Seven-Colored Daoist becomes the world-owner.
- **Remaining traces:** The entire Cave World IS this trace. The Realm-Sealing Grand Array (placed later) is the seal mechanism. Every cultivator inside is, unknowingly, the Daoist's livestock.
- **Canon confidence:** C5 (Book 11 revelation; CANON_RI_COMPLETE_WORLD.md L04)

### E03. The Seven-Colored Daoist Creates the Three Souls and Seven Spirits
- **Date:** ~−100,000 BW (after E02)
- **Location:** The Cave World
- **Participants:** Seven-Colored Daoist
- **Consequences:** The Daoist fragments himself into Three Souls and Seven Spirits — these are his "fragments" that reincarnate. Known fragments:
  - **Situ Nan** (司徒南) = Green Soul (2nd-Gen Vermilion Bird; transmits Underworld Ascension Method + Vermilion Bird Burning Heaven Art to Wang Lin)
  - **Qing Shui** (清水) = Slaughter Soul (Wang Lin's dao-companion Li Muwan's mentor; Wang Lin later consumes Qing Shui's Black Sword to complete Slaughter Essence)
  - **Tan Lang** (贪狼) = Yellow Soul
  - **Xie Qing** (邪清) = Third Soul
  - (others not fully enumerated in canon)
- **Remaining traces:** Situ Nan is inside the Heaven-Defying Bead when Wang Lin finds it. Qing Shui's legacy reaches Wang Lin through Li Muwan. The other souls reincarnate across the Cave World.
- **Canon confidence:** C5 (CANON_RI_COMPLETE_WORLD.md Seven-Colored Daoist entry)

### E04. The Realm-Sealing Supreme and the Heaven-Defying Bead
- **Date:** ~−100,000 BW (after E03)
- **Location:** The Sealed Realm
- **Participants:** Seven-Colored Daoist, Realm-Sealing Supreme (封界至尊)
- **Consequences:** The Seven-Colored Daoist bestows the Heaven-Defying Pearl (逆天珠) to the Realm-Sealing Supreme as proof of authority over the Inner Realm. The bead is the core of a 9-part transcendent artifact. It will eventually be sent back through time to Wang Lin.
- **Remaining traces:** The bead is inside the Heng Yue Sect's stone bead when Wang Lin finds it at age 16 (E12).
- **Canon confidence:** C5 (CANON_RI_COMPLETE_ITEMS.md item #1)

### E05. The Realm-Sealing Grand Array is Placed
- **Date:** ~−100,000 BW (after E04)
- **Location:** The boundary between the Sealed Realm and the Outer Realm
- **Participants:** Seven-Colored Daoist (creator), Heaven-Splitting Axe (开天斧 — the array's sentient spirit)
- **Consequences:** The Sealed Realm (inner half of the Cave World) is sealed AGAIN. The array's spirit is the Heaven-Splitting Axe. The seal suppresses Third-Step cultivation — only "Heaven Blight" (quasi-Third-Step) cultivators can squeeze through.
- **Remaining traces:** The array is active throughout Wang Lin's lifetime. Wang Lin eventually borrows the Axe's power to kill Outer-Realm Third-Step cultivators, then dissolves the array after killing the Seven-Colored Daoist.
- **Canon confidence:** C5 (CANON_RI_COMPLETE_WORLD.md L05)

### E06. The Tu Si Ancient God Era
- **Date:** ~−50,000 BW (estimated)
- **Location:** The Land of the Ancient God (inside the Cave World)
- **Participants:** Tu Si (涂司) — an Ancient God-tier cultivator
- **Consequences:** Tu Si leaves behind a memory legacy inside the Ancient God's body. The legacy contains the Ancient God Tactic (古神诀) — the body-cultivation art whose essence is "plunder everything." This is the origin of Wang Lin's Ancient God track.
- **Remaining traces:** Wang Lin inherits Tu Si's legacy at Ch. 190. His hair turns white after absorbing it. He reconstructs his body as a 1-Star Ancient God at Ch. 199.
- **Canon confidence:** C5 (CANON_RI_COMPLETE_TECHNIQUES.md Ancient God Tactic entry)

### E07. The Bai Fan Era
- **Date:** ~−10,000 BW (estimated)
- **Location:** The Cave World (multiple locations)
- **Participants:** Bai Fan (白帆) — an ancient cultivator
- **Consequences:** Bai Fan leaves behind the Six Paths Triple Arts, including Mountain Crumble (the technique Wang Lin uses to manipulate mountains via divine sense). Bai Fan's inheritance becomes one of Wang Lin's key combat tools.
- **Remaining traces:** Wang Lin learns Mountain Crumble at Ch. 1105. It triggers his breakthrough to mid Nirvana Cleanser (Ch. 1102) and completes his Karma Domain.
- **Canon confidence:** C5 (CANON_RI_COMPLETE_TECHNIQUES.md Mountain Crumble entry)

## ERA 2 — Wang Lin's Early Life (0 to +16 BW)

### E40. Situ Nan Is Betrayed — Physical Body Destroyed
- **Date:** ~−5,000 BW (estimated; well before Wang Lin's birth)
- **Location:** Vermilion Bird Starfield
- **Participants:** Situ Nan (2nd-Gen Vermilion Bird, Green Soul), Third-Gen Vermilion Bird Master, Tan Lang (Yellow Soul)
- **Consequences:** After Situ Nan sealed himself using the Cultivation Star Crystal, the Third-Gen Vermilion Bird and Tan Lang launched a sneak attack, destroying Situ Nan's physical body. His remnant soul fled into the Heaven-Defying Bead. The 14th-Gen Vermilion Bird later severed three of his own fingers and fled at the mere mention of Situ Nan's name.
- **Remaining traces:** Situ Nan's remnant soul is inside the Heaven-Defying Bead when Wang Lin finds it. The Vermilion Bird lineage is fractured — only Lu Yun (5th-Gen) maintains the true line secretly.
- **Canon confidence:** C4 (CANON_RI_COMPLETE_WORLD.md N81-N82)


### E08. Wang Lin is Born
- **Date:** Year 0 (Wang Lin's birth)
- **Location:** Wang Family Village, Zhao Country, Planet Suzaku, Brilliant Void Star System, Sealed Realm, Cave World
- **Participants:** Wang Tianshui (father), Zhou Tingsu (mother), Wang Lin (newborn)
- **Consequences:** A mortal child is born. His parents call him "Tie Zhu" (铁柱 — "Iron Pillar") to ward off bad fortune. He is an ordinary mortal with no apparent spiritual roots.
- **Remaining traces:** Wang Family Village exists as a starting location. The player can visit his birthplace.
- **Canon confidence:** C5

### E09. Wang Lin's Childhood
- **Date:** Years 0-15
- **Location:** Wang Family Village
- **Participants:** Wang Lin, his family, Fourth Uncle (Wang Tianshan)
- **Consequences:** Wang Lin grows up as a mortal. Fourth Uncle favors him. Wang Lin is intelligent but sickly.
- **Remaining traces:** The Wang Family Village NPCs remember "Tie Zhu" as a child.
- **Canon confidence:** C5

### E10. The Heng Yue Sect Recruitment
- **Date:** Year 15-16
- **Location:** Heng Yue Sect (恒岳派), Zhao Country
- **Participants:** Wang Lin, Heng Yue Sect elders, Wang Tianshui
- **Consequences:** Wang Lin is tested for spiritual roots. His roots are poor (lowest tier). He is accepted as a legacy disciple through Fourth Uncle's connection, but is bullied and given menial tasks.
- **Remaining traces:** Heng Yue Sect exists as the starting sect. The player can find the stone bead (containing the Heaven-Defying Bead) where Wang Lin found it.
- **Canon confidence:** C5

### E11. Wang Lin Discovers the Stone Bead
- **Date:** Year 16, Ch. 1-8 (donghua S1E2)
- **Location:** Heng Yue Sect — the cliff where a dead bird lies
- **Participants:** Wang Lin
- **Consequences:** Wang Lin finds a stone bead inside a dead bird under a cliff. The bead is the Heaven-Defying Bead (sent back through time by his future clone Lu Mo via Dream Dao). The bead contains Situ Nan (the Green Soul fragment of the Seven-Colored Daoist). Wang Lin does not yet know the bead's nature.
- **Remaining traces:** The cliff and dead-bird location are a landmark. The bead is now Wang Lin's (and stays with him to end of RI).
- **Canon confidence:** C5 (CANON_RI_COMPLETE_ITEMS.md item #1)

## ERA 3 — The Zhao Country Arc (Year 16 to ~25)

### E12. Situ Nan Awakens, Transmits the Underworld Ascension Method
- **Date:** Year ~17, Ch. 86 (donghua S1 EP8)
- **Location:** Inside the Heaven-Defying Bead's interior chamber
- **Participants:** Wang Lin, Situ Nan (remnant soul inside the bead)
- **Consequences:** Situ Nan transmits the Underworld Ascension Method (黄泉升窍诀) — a Ji Realm cultivation method. Wang Lin begins cultivating toward Foundation Establishment. The method produces cold-attribute Blue Flames (Ch. 121) and Red Lightning / Ji Realm Divine Sense (Ch. 127).
- **Remaining traces:** Wang Lin's Ji Realm divine sense is the foundation of his entire combat style. The Underworld Ascension Method becomes his early-game engine.
- **Canon confidence:** C5 (CANON_RI_COMPLETE_TECHNIQUES.md Underworld Ascension Method entry)

### E13. The Teng Clan Massacre of the Wang Family
- **Date:** Year ~18-20
- **Location:** Wang Family Village, Zhao Country
- **Participants:** Teng Huayuan (腾华元 — Teng Clan patriarch), Wang Tianshui, Zhou Tingsu, Wang family members
- **Consequences:** Teng Huayuan slaughters the Wang family to take revenge on Wang Lin (who had killed a Teng clan member). Wang Lin's parents are killed; their souls are trapped in Teng Huayuan's soul flag. This is the defining trauma of Wang Lin's life — it triggers his Slaughter Dao and his lifelong pursuit of power.
- **Remaining traces:** The ruined Wang Family Village. Wang Lin's parents' souls are eventually rescued by Situ Nan into the Heaven-Defying Bead, then released/reincarnated later.
- **Canon confidence:** C5 (CANON_RI_COMPLETE_WORLD.md Teng Huayuan entry)

### E14. Wang Lin's Revenge on the Teng Clan
- **Date:** Year ~20-22
- **Location:** Zhao Country / Teng Clan territory
- **Participants:** Wang Lin, Teng Huayuan, Teng Clan members
- **Consequences:** Wang Lin systematically exterminates the Teng Clan. He kills Teng Huayuan and rescues his parents' souls from the soul flag. This is his first major killing arc — the foundation of his Slaughter Essence (later completed at Ch. 1622).
- **Remaining traces:** The Teng Clan is annihilated. Their territory becomes a ruin. Wang Lin's reputation as "Soul Devourer" begins.
- **Canon confidence:** C5

### E15. Core Formation in the Sea of Devils
- **Date:** Year ~22-25
- **Location:** Sea of Devils (魔海), Zhao Country region
- **Participants:** Wang Lin, various Sea of Devils cultivators and beasts
- **Consequences:** Wang Lin reaches Core Formation (结丹) in the Sea of Devils. He uses the Cold Dan (cold core) from the Underworld Ascension Method. His Golden Core forms. He begins to establish his reputation.
- **Remaining traces:** The Sea of Devils is a mid-tier dangerous zone the player can explore. Wang Lin's Cold Dan technique is learnable.
- **Canon confidence:** C5

## ERA 4 — The Suzaku Inheritance Arc (Year ~25 to ~100)

### E41. Wang Lin Awakens the Ji Realm
- **Date:** Year ~17, Ch. 127
- **Location:** Zhao Country
- **Participants:** Wang Lin
- **Consequences:** Wang Lin's spiritual energy undergoes a qualitative transformation into the Ji Realm (Extreme Realm) divine sense, triggered by the trauma of the Wang Clan's annihilation combined with the Underworld Ascension Method's catalytic effect. The Ji Realm becomes his signature killing ability through Foundation Establishment. Also produces Blue Flames (Ch. 121). The Ji Realm later conflicts with Nascent Soul breakthrough, requiring the Avatar workaround. Eventually absorbed into Ji Thunder as the 7th accompanying thunder (Ch. 1368).
- **Remaining traces:** Ji Realm divine sense persists throughout Wang Lin's career. The trauma site where the awakening occurred.
- **Canon confidence:** C5 (CANON_RI_COMPLETE_TECHNIQUES.md, Ch. 127)

### E42. Wang Lin Discovers His Soul-Devourer Nature
- **Date:** Year ~17, Ch. 119
- **Location:** Zhao Country
- **Participants:** Wang Lin
- **Consequences:** Wang Lin discovers his innate Soul-Devourer nature. His soul contains a Soul Gem, granting him the ability to command wandering souls. This is the foundation of his soul-based combat style and his title "Soul Devourer." The Soul Gem later devours the Ancient Thunder Dragon's second origin soul (Ch. 650), absorbs Heavenly Flame (Ch. 1021), and eventually transforms into an Ancient Order Soul — making him the "Ancient Ancestor's Fourth Child."
- **Remaining traces:** Wandering souls in the Sea of Devils and beyond still respond to Soul-Devourers.
- **Canon confidence:** C5 (CANON_RI_COMPLETE_TECHNIQUES.md, Ch. 119)

### E43. Wang Lin Passes the Restriction Mountain Trial
- **Date:** Year ~19-22, Ch. ~179-180
- **Location:** Land of the Ancient God — Restriction Mountain (Level 3 of Chaotic Broken Stars)
- **Participants:** Wang Lin, Tu Si's legacy (Restriction refining method reward)
- **Consequences:** Wang Lin spends 7 years studying ancient restrictions at the Restriction Mountain. He becomes only the 4th person in history to complete the trial. He develops the Illusionary Circle technique (wave-based restriction analysis without needing to see the restriction). He receives the Restriction Flag Refining Method from Tu Si (99,999 restrictions per flag). His hair turns white. This is the foundation of his restriction specialization that persists to endgame.
- **Remaining traces:** The Restriction Mountain still exists as a trial site. The Illusionary Circle is Wang Lin's signature analysis technique. The three Restriction Flags he later forges are deployable formation-arrays.
- **Canon confidence:** C5 (CANON_RI_COMPLETE_TECHNIQUES.md, Ch. ~179-180)


### E16. The Foreign Battleground and Soul Devourer
- **Date:** Year ~25-40
- **Location:** Foreign Battleground (外战场)
- **Participants:** Wang Lin, various foreign cultivators
- **Consequences:** Wang Lin participates in the Foreign Battleground. He earns the title "Soul Devourer" for his habit of devouring enemy souls. He begins to build his combat reputation beyond Zhao Country.
- **Remaining traces:** The Foreign Battleground exists as a PvP-enabled zone.
- **Canon confidence:** C5

### E17. Inheriting Tu Si's Ancient God Legacy
- **Date:** Year ~40, Ch. 190-199
- **Location:** The Land of the Ancient God
- **Participants:** Wang Lin, Tu Si's memory legacy
- **Consequences:** Wang Lin inherits the Ancient God Tactic from Tu Si's body. His hair turns white. He reconstructs his body as a 1-Star Ancient God (Ch. 199). This is the start of his parallel body-cultivation track — he is now a hybrid (Qi Cultivator + Ancient God).
- **Remaining traces:** The Land of the Ancient God is a high-tier ruin. The Ancient God Tactic is Wang Lin's signature body-cultivation art.
- **Canon confidence:** C5 (CANON_RI_COMPLETE_TECHNIQUES.md Ancient God Tactic entry)

### E18. Soul Formation via the Life-Death Domain
- **Date:** Year ~60-80
- **Location:** A mortal village (Wang Lin lives as a mortal among mortals)
- **Participants:** Wang Lin, mortal villagers
- **Consequences:** Wang Lin reaches Soul Formation (化神) by comprehending the Life-Death Domain. He lives as a mortal among mortals — experiencing birth, aging, sickness, death firsthand. This is the foundation of his Life-Death Essence (later his 4th Essence).
- **Remaining traces:** The mortal village where Wang Lin lived is a pilgrimage site. His Life-Death Domain technique is learnable.
- **Canon confidence:** C5

### E19. The Cloud Sky Sect Era
- **Date:** Year ~80-100
- **Location:** Cloud Sky Sect (云天宗)
- **Participants:** Wang Lin (as sect master), Cloud Sky Sect disciples
- **Consequences:** Wang Lin becomes the master of the Cloud Sky Sect. He begins to build his faction. This is a relatively peaceful period in his life.
- **Remaining traces:** Cloud Sky Sect exists as a mid-tier sect the player can visit.
- **Canon confidence:** C5

## ERA 5 — The Heavenly Fate Sect Arc (Year ~100 to ~300)

### E44. Wang Lin Raids the Suzaku Tomb
- **Date:** Year ~25-30
- **Location:** Suzaku Tomb / Cultivation Planet Crystal — underground, Planet Suzaku
- **Participants:** Wang Lin, Piao Nanzi / Lou Hou (scattered demon sealed within)
- **Consequences:** Wang Lin enters the multi-floor tomb within Planet Suzaku containing the Cultivation Planet Crystal (the planet's seal mechanism). He finds the Half-Moon Blade. He encounters the scattered demon Piao Nanzi/Lou Hou who is sealed here. This is Wang Lin's first major tomb-raiding arc and introduces him to the planet-level cosmology.
- **Remaining traces:** The Suzaku Tomb is a ruin the player can explore. The Cultivation Planet Crystal is Planet Suzaku's core seal mechanism.
- **Canon confidence:** C4 (CANON_RI_COMPLETE_WORLD.md L52)

### E45. Wang Lin Inherits the Soul Refining Sect from Dun Tian
- **Date:** Year ~50-70, Ch. 384
- **Location:** Soul Refining Sect, Pilu Kingdom, Planet Suzaku
- **Participants:** Wang Lin, Dun Tian (Soul Refining Sect ancestor), Nian Tian (Dun Tian's senior brother)
- **Consequences:** Dun Tian gives Wang Lin three gifts: (1) helped Wang Lin's clone reach Nascent Soul peak, (2) helped Wang Lin's true body reach 3-Star Ancient God, (3) bestowed the Ten Billion Soul Banner and Soul Refining Sect inheritance. Wang Lin takes Dun Tian to seize Immortal Jades for his Soul Transformation breakthrough. Dun Tian then erases his own consciousness to become a soul within the Soul Banner, on Wang Lin's promise to elevate the Soul Refining Sect to 6th-level. Wang Lin learns Soul Refining/Extracting/Sealing methods.
- **Remaining traces:** The Soul Refining Sect survives. The Billion Soul Banner is Wang Lin's signature soul weapon. Dun Tian exists as a soul within it.
- **Canon confidence:** C5 (CANON_RI_COMPLETE_WORLD.md N23; CANON_RI_COMPLETE_TECHNIQUES.md, Ch. 384)

### E46. Wang Lin Becomes the 6th-Gen Vermilion Bird Divine Emperor
- **Date:** Year ~70-90
- **Location:** Vermilion Bird Starfield / Four Divine Sect
- **Participants:** Wang Lin, Lu Yun (5th-Gen Vermilion Bird, secretly Heng Yue's Huang Long Zhenren), Azure Dragon Divine Emperor
- **Consequences:** Wang Lin inherits the Vermilion Bird Divine Emperor position, becoming the 6th generation. Lu Yun (Wang Lin's secret benefactor who had given him the Vermilion Bird Sequence as a Heng Yue Sect disciple) was the 5th-Gen. Wang Lin absorbs Vermilion Bird fire energy, triggering his fire-element cultivation track. He eventually returns the rein to the Azure Dragon Divine Emperor.
- **Remaining traces:** The Vermilion Bird Divine Sect survives. The Four Divine Sect recognizes Wang Lin's legacy.
- **Canon confidence:** C5 (CANON_RI_COMPLETE_WORLD.md N25, F11)

### E47. Wang Lin Kills Sun Zhenwei, Becomes Cloud Sky Sect Master
- **Date:** Year ~85-95
- **Location:** Cloud Sky Sect, Chu Country, Planet Suzaku
- **Participants:** Wang Lin, Sun Zhenwei (suitor), Li Muwan, Chen Bailiang (elder, killed)
- **Consequences:** Li Muwan agrees to marry Sun Zhenwei in front of Wang Lin — to shatter her own heart (a cultivation method). At the wedding, Wang Lin kills Sun Zhenwei and tries to take Li Muwan back. He then kills Chen Bailiang and becomes the Cloud Sky Sect leader. He later hands the position to Li Muwan. This event establishes Wang Lin's willingness to defy entire sects for Li Muwan.
- **Remaining traces:** The Cloud Sky Sect survives. Li Muwan serves as Sect Master. The wedding site is a landmark.
- **Canon confidence:** C4 (CANON_RI_COMPLETE_WORLD.md N146-N147)

### E97. Wang Lin Kills Hong Die — The Kunji Whip Changes Hands
- **Date:** Year ~80-100
- **Location:** Planet Suzaku region
- **Participants:** Wang Lin, Hong Die (killed), Qian Feng (Tian Yu Sect, original giver of whip)
- **Consequences:** Hong Die, who had received the Kunji Whip from Qian Feng (Tian Yu Sect heavy treasure) for her battle with Wang Lin, is killed. Wang Lin takes the Kunji Whip (originally Red Butterfly's). The whip directly attacks the primordial/origin soul at warp speed. It is later burned by Ghostly Sky Fire in the Demon Spirit Land, nourished by Wang Lin's Karma Concept, and becomes the Karma Whip.
- **Remaining traces:** The Karma Whip is the evolved form of Hong Die's Kunji Whip.
- **Canon confidence:** C5 (CANON_RI_COMPLETE_ITEMS.md, Kunji Whip entry)


### E20. Wang Lin Joins the Heavenly Fate Sect
- **Date:** Year ~100, Ch. 493 (S1 EP106)
- **Location:** Heavenly Fate Sect (天运宗) on Planet Tian Yun
- **Participants:** Wang Lin, All-Seer (全知者 — sect master), Tianyunzi (All-Seer's clone)
- **Consequences:** Wang Lin joins the Heavenly Fate Sect as the 7th purple disciple. The All-Seer transmits the Nine Cycle Celestial Refining Tactic (九转炼仙诀) to Wang Lin — but this is a TROJAN HORSE. The art creates an immortal-power spiral that the All-Seer can hijack to possess Wang Lin's body.
- **Remaining traces:** The Heavenly Fate Sect's 7 color divisions (red/orange/yellow/green/blue/cyan/purple) exist as sub-factions. The Nine Cycle Celestial Refining Tactic is learnable but carries the possession risk.
- **Canon confidence:** C5 (CANON_RI_COMPLETE_TECHNIQUES.md Nine Cycle Celestial Refining Tactic entry)

### E21. The All-Seer's Possession Plot
- **Date:** Year ~200-300
- **Location:** Heavenly Fate Sect
- **Participants:** Wang Lin, All-Seer
- **Consequences:** The All-Seer plots to possess Wang Lin's body to escape his own karmic doom. Wang Lin's body is uniquely suitable (Ancient God + Ji Realm + Heaven-Defying Bead fusion). Wang Lin eventually discovers the plot.
- **Remaining traces:** The All-Seer's seven color-division disciples are scattered across Planet Tian Yun. The possession plot is the central conflict of this arc.
- **Canon confidence:** C5

### E22. Wang Lin Kills the All-Seer
- **Date:** Year ~300
- **Location:** Heavenly Fate Sect
- **Participants:** Wang Lin, All-Seer
- **Consequences:** Wang Lin kills the All-Seer at the end of the Sealed Realm arc. The possession plot is reversed — Wang Lin devours the All-Seer instead. This is the defeat of the mortal-scheme-layer antagonist.
- **Remaining traces:** The Heavenly Fate Sect is without its master. Wang Lin inherits certain of the All-Seer's resources.
- **Canon confidence:** C5

## ERA 6 — The Allheaven Star System Arc (Year ~300 to ~500)

### E48. Wang Lin Creates His First Immortal Guard, Du Jian
- **Date:** Year ~120-150
- **Location:** Heavenly Fate Sect / Alliance Star System
- **Participants:** Wang Lin, Du Jian (Heavenly Fate Sect disciple), a Ghost King
- **Consequences:** Wang Lin creates his first Immortal Guard by fusing Du Jian (a Heavenly Fate Sect disciple) with a Ghost King's soul, producing a hybrid entity at Yin Void level. Du Jian helps Wang Lin escape the Chaotic Expanse. He later protects Wang Ping (Wang Lin's mortal son) for years. Du Jian is eventually destroyed by the Blood Ancestor.
- **Remaining traces:** Du Jian is dead. Wang Ping remembers the guard who protected him.
- **Canon confidence:** C5 (CANON_RI_COMPLETE_TECHNIQUES.md, Immortal Guard entry)

### E49. Wang Lin Learns the Celestial Slaughter Art (The Trap)
- **Date:** Year ~110, Ch. 504
- **Location:** Heavenly Fate Sect, Planet Tian Yun
- **Participants:** Wang Lin, All-Seer, Tianyunzi (All-Seer's clone / Boundary Compass spirit)
- **Consequences:** Wang Lin obtains the Celestial Slaughter Art from Tianyunzi's clone (on behalf of the All-Seer). The art condenses life force from slaughters into "life seals" that protect the user. The trap: after gathering exactly 10 million life seals, the seals envelop the user into a "true-life seal" — giving the All-Seer a backdoor to possess Wang Lin's body. Wang Lin eventually discovers this, forcibly expels the art into his first Immortal Guard, and re-cultivates it properly after obtaining the Slaughter Origin.
- **Remaining traces:** The Celestial Slaughter Art's malicious legacy is a cautionary tale about cultivation techniques with hidden possession mechanisms.
- **Canon confidence:** C5 (CANON_RI_COMPLETE_TECHNIQUES.md, Ch. 504)

### E50. Wang Lin Lives a Mortal Life with His Son Wang Ping
- **Date:** Year ~200-250, Ch. 701
- **Location:** A mortal village (unnamed)
- **Participants:** Wang Lin (living as mortal), Wang Ping (mortal son), mortal wife
- **Consequences:** Wang Lin lives as a mortal for a full lifetime, experiencing birth, aging, sickness, and death firsthand. He raises a son, Wang Ping. After his mortal wife dies of old age and Wang Ping grows up, Wang Lin finally understands "what life means." This comprehension pushes him to Ascendant mid-stage. This is his second major mortal-life comprehension arc (the first was E18 for Soul Formation).
- **Remaining traces:** Wang Ping exists as a mortal NPC. The village where Wang Lin lived is a pilgrimage site.
- **Canon confidence:** C5 (CANON_RI_COMPLETE_TECHNIQUES.md, Ch. 701; CANON_RI_COMPLETE_WORLD.md N111)

### E51. Wang Lin Becomes the Third Heaven-Defying Cultivator
- **Date:** Year ~250, Ch. 599
- **Location:** Alliance Star System
- **Participants:** Wang Lin, Heaven and Earth (divine retribution)
- **Consequences:** Wang Lin forms his own Dao and breaks through to Ascendant, becoming only the third Heaven-Defying Cultivator in the Cave World's history. This incites divine retribution (Heaven's punishment for defying its laws). Zhou Yi's Ascendant Crystal is crucial for surviving the breakthrough difficulty. This establishes Wang Lin as a heaven-defying anomaly in the cosmology.
- **Remaining traces:** The scar of divine retribution at the breakthrough site.
- **Canon confidence:** C5 (CANON_RI_COMPLETE_TECHNIQUES.md, Ch. 599)

### E108. Wang Lin Creates His Second Immortal Guard — Ta Shan
- **Date:** Year ~200-400
- **Location:** Xianxuan Tribe territory
- **Participants:** Wang Lin, Ta Shan (Xianxuan Tribe warrior)
- **Consequences:** Wang Lin creates his second Immortal Guard from Ta Shan, pushing him to peak Yang Real stage. Ta Shan is unique — he is the only guard to ever regain freedom when an ancestral elder shatters his slave seal. His cultivation eventually reaches the Celestial Immortal realm independently.
- **Remaining traces:** Ta Shan is alive and free, at Celestial Immortal realm.
- **Canon confidence:** C5 (CANON_RI_COMPLETE_TECHNIQUES.md, Immortal Guard entry)


### E23. The Demon Spirit Land and Yao Xixue
- **Date:** Year ~300, Ch. 491+

## WHAT THE PLAYER ENCOUNTERS (the "remaining traces" column)

This is the key column for the Forge mod. Every event leaves traces. The player enters a world where:

1. **The Cave World ownership** — Seven-Colored Daoist (pre-Year ~1500) or Wang Lin (post-Year ~1500).
2. **Wang Family Village** — populated (Year 0-20) or ruin (post-Teng massacre).
3. **Heng Yue Sect** — active with stone-bead cliff landmark (Lu Yun/Huang Long Zhenren is secretly 5th-Gen Vermilion Bird, per E46).
4. **Sea of Devils** — mid-tier dangerous zone (Core Formation breakthrough site, E15).
5. **Land of the Ancient God** — high-tier ruin with Restriction Mountain (E43; 4th-person trial, still active).
6. **Suzaku Tomb** — multi-floor dungeon with Cultivation Planet Crystal (E44).
7. **Soul Refining Sect** — Dun Tian's soul in the Billion Soul Banner (E45).
8. **Cloud Sky Sect** — Li Muwan as Sect Master (E47); wedding site landmark.
9. **Heavenly Fate Sect** — with or without the All-Seer; Celestial Slaughter Art trap site (E49).
10. **The Sealed Realm boundary** — scarred warzone (E26); now sealed by Unnamed Wheel Formation (E70) instead of the original Realm-Sealing Array.
11. **The Heaven-Splitting Axe's former location** — landmark at the old Realm-Sealing boundary (E71).
12. **Seven-Colored Realm** — high-tier pocket-world zone (E58).
13. **The Immortal Monarch's Cave Mansion** — high-tier dungeon (E57).
14. **Autumn Orchid Valley** — Xie Qing's burial site (E73); Dao-comprehension landmark.
15. **Dong Lin Sect ruins** — Tianyunzi's handwriting; Dong Lin Pool meditation site (E72, E85).
16. **The Ancient Immortal Domain** — endgame zone with Thunder Immortal World.
17. **The Immortal Astral Continent** — Xiangang Continent with arc-shaped platform (E82); Dao Devil Sect ruins (E83); Great Soul Sect (E98-E99).
18. **The 9 Heaven Trampling Bridges** — 1st-4th and 5th-7th crossed (E101-E104, E92); 8th uncrossed (E105); 9th untouched.
19. **Blood Planet** — ruin of Blood Ancestor Yao Xinghai (E94).
20. **Scatter Thunder Clan territory** — devastated; Thunder Toad sacrifice site (E68).

The player's starting era determines which traces are present and their state. The default start is Year 16 (Wang Lin's era, just after he finds the bead) — the player experiences the world as Wang Lin's contemporary.
