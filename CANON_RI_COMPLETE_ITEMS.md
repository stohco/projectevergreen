## 1. CORE TREASURES (the "cheat" artifacts)

### Heaven-Defying Bead / Pearl (逆天珠 · Nì Tiān Zhū)
- **Chapter obtained:** Ch. 8 (donghua S1E2); truly recognized as Wang Lin's later
- **Chapter lost:** retained to end (fuses with primordial spirit)
- **Category:** core / utility
- **Grade:** transcendent (above celestial / pseudo-immortal; "root cause of the great war in the Ancient Immortal Domain")
- **What it does:** Bead with the Five Elements pattern. Originally 9 parts; the bead is its core. Interior door leads to a chamber where time runs 10× outside. Used to recognize-master Wang Lin after Lu Mo (his clone) blasts it open via Dream Dao. Once Five Elements perfected the master truly owns it. Stores Li Muwan's Nascent Soul after her body perishes. Reputed to contain Third-Step divine abilities inside.
- **How obtained:** found by Wang Lin as a youth inside the Heng Yue Sect stone bead; revealed later to have been sent back through time by his future clone Lu Mo via Dream Dao (originally bestowed by Seven-Colored Immortal Venerable to Realm-Sealing Supreme)
- **Special properties:** sentient / destiny-bound / fused with primordial spirit / time dilation interior / cross-novel artifact (also wielded by Su Ming / Xuan Zang)
- **Current status:** retained (becomes one with Wang Lin's primordial spirit)
- **Canon confidence:** 5

### Realm-Defining Compass (界定 Compass)
- **Chapter obtained:** borrowed by Lu Mo from Old Man Miēshēng, then used to crack open the Heaven-Defying Bead
- **Category:** core / divination
- **Grade:** quasi-Third-Step treasure
- **What it does:** Compass whose core IS the Heaven-Defying Pearl. Used to define and shatter realms; Lu Mo borrowed it to blast open the bead. Has a "Realm-Sealing" function.
- **How obtained:** borrowed from Old Man Miēshēng (灭生老人) by Lu Mo clone
- **Special properties:** paired with Heaven-Defying Pearl; one-time borrow (returned)
- **Current status:** returned to Old Man Miēshēng
- **Canon confidence:** 4

## CROSS-REFERENCE NOTES

   - Bronze Mirror with Time Domain → sold Ch. 664
   - Sword with Teleportation Spell → sold Ch. 665

   (Wang Lin later reacquires a teleportation sword from Bai Zhan.)

3. **God-Slaying War Chariot** set of 3: low→given to Situ Nan; mid→unsealed into Lei Ji (Thunder Beast companion); high→butterfly-soul, destroyed & restored.

4. **Avatar fusion order** (per Baidu):
   - Cultivator Clone (fused back, removed 30-yr-lifespan drawback via Ji Realm conflict)
   - Celestial Body + Thunder Body → fused → Immortal Celestial Body
   - Ancient Demon + Ancient Devil → fused → Ancient One
   - Void Avatar → completely fused during Ancient Path battle
   - Slaughter Origin + Miemie Origin → Lu Mo (Fourth-Step clone)
   - Slaughter Origin + Thunder Origin → Annihilating Thunder
   - Five Elements Origins (×5) → Five Elements True Body

5. **The 14 Origins** Wang Lin possesses (per Baidu):
   - Substantial (5): Metal, Wood, Water, Fire, Earth — each condenses a True Body, can fuse into Five Elements True Body
   - Substantial (1): Thunder — has formed a True Body
   - Virtual (4): Life-Death, Cause-Effect, True-False, Reincarnation — power equally formidable, represent four realms, have not formed true bodies
   - Special (4): Taichu, Miemie, Restriction, Slaughter — each has formed its own True Body

6. **Heaven-Defying Pearl cross-novel thread:** Originally 9 parts; the Pearl is the most important. Once used by Xuan Zang (Dao-Wuya cultivator) as he traversed the Vast Expanse. Su Ming (Pursuit of Truth) successfully seized it after possession and controlled it. Before Su Ming died, he used it to locate his friend the Bald Feather Crane (Tian Yunzi), thus arriving in the Inverse Dust Realm. This is the cross-novel link between Pursuit of Truth → Renegade Immortal.
