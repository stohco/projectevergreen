# Ergenverse — Complete Systems Design
> Every workstation is a physical block you place in the world — not a hotkey menu.
## 13 Crafting Systems (all ≤12 second crafts)
1. Alchemy Furnace (炼丹炉) — 5 ingredient slots + fuel + output. Property-based.
2. Artifact Forge (炼器台) — 2-4 materials + 6 modes. Property-based.
3. Talisman Desk (符箓桌) — paper + ink + intent. Like vanilla loom.
4. Formation Platform (阵法台) — 3x3 grid of components.
5. Restriction Flag (禁制旗) — 9 layers × 11,111, 5-second sessions.
6-13: Puppet Forge, Storage Ring, Jade Slip, Soul Altar, Body Cauldron, Cave Dwelling, Beast Whip, Meditation Mat.
## GUI Design Principles
1. Every workstation is a physical block. No hotkey menus.
2. Right-click to open — same as vanilla furnace.
3. Slot-based input — drag items, not click buttons.
4. Progress bars — visual fill like vanilla furnace.
5. No tabs or web-style elements.
6. Tooltips on hover — properties visible if perception tier is high enough.
## Already Decided (from worklog)
- Property-based + 14 known pill patterns (both fixed recipes AND procedural discovery)
- Realm-gated crafting (mortals can't channel Qi)
- Deterministic pill type + random explosion/quality (stability × potency × skill)
- Fire grade affects yield/quality, not duration
