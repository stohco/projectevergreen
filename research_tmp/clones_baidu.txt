The Wang Lin Clone is a manifestation of the core ability of the protagonist Wang Lin in the fantasy xianxia novel Renegade Immortal by Er Gen , enabling strategic deployment through the creation of multiple clones. His three major clones include the Cultivator Clone, the Divine Path Clone, and the Slaughter-Silence Clone. The Cultivator Clone primarily assists the main body in cultivation. The Divine Path Clone once studied alchemy on behalf of Wang Lin and helped him escape danger during the Wedding Snatching Incident. The Slaughter-Silence Clone was formed by the fusion of the Killing Origin and the Silent Extinction Origin. Its power surpasses that of the main body and it was created and sent back tens of thousands of years in the past for the purpose of resurrecting Li Muwan . After Li Muwan's resurrection, Wang Lin separated from his clones, and the afterword suggests that the clones may have remained with different female characters.
