# Ji Realm
URL: https://xian-ni.fandom.com/wiki/Ji_Realm

JI Realm is one of the three great realms besides Shi Realm and Dao Realm. Ji Realm is a realm that represents Extreme Force and Death.

The Ji Realm is a unique path of cultivation that revolves around lightning. A cultivator who cultivates the Ji Realm will be unmatched by any within their cultivation realm, an example being Wang Lin who killed three Core Formation cultivators with it as soon as he breakthrough to Core Formation.

There is one significant flaw in cultivating the Ji Realm and that is the realm imposes a limit on the future cultivation of the cultivator. This limit differs depending on the person, for Wang Lin this was Core Formation, meaning that theoretically he could never breakthrough to Nascent Soul.

However, the restriction of the Ji Realm can be bypassed by forming an avatar and having it cultivate without the Ji Realm as shown by Wang Lin. It can also be separated from the user later on and refined into a treasure for external use. Wang Lin used it to help form his Thunder Essence.

It is unknown how one specifically cultivates the Ji Realm, but Wang Lin unknowingly obtains it when he broke through to Foundation Establishment.

Wang Ling got his Ji realm :

“Back in the Jue Ming valley, a sliver of this Ji Realm appeared in my body. You were still sleeping then, so you didn’t see it. Then my body was destroyed and you saved my soul before going back to sleep again. In the foreign battleground, my Ji Realm reached its completion.”

Trivia[]

• The Ji Realm was partially imitated by a technique known as the Death Spell, which allows one to turn their spiritual sense into an attack.

• There are 2 type of Ji Realm cultivators, the first is to have part of the Ji Realm power in their qi like the Core Formation realm Wang Lin who had the Ji Realm integrated with his qi, the second is the True Ji Realm, it acts at the spiritual level like the 2 other great realms.
