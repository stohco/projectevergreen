Bloodlines[]

• Ancient Bloodline (Ancient Clan)
• Common Royal (Former): Royal Blood in the Cave World mean that your bloodline purity is close to the Ancestor that left the Ancient Order Lineage in the Cave World. The Ancestor/Ye Mo's bloodline belongs to the High class so Wang Lin has a not so low bloodline but it's not the best because Ye Mo's original bloodline as an Ancient Clansman was common in the Ancient Clan's Imperial Family but with the 9 drop of blood and one drop of soul blood, he was able to obtain a bloodline higher than the Royal Bloodline.

• Ancestor: After completing the Three Destructions and Seven Tribulations of all three branches of the Ancient Clan, Wang Lin received two more drops of soul blood from the Ancient Ancestor's statue. In addition to the drop he received back in the Cave World, this transformed his bloodline to the level of the Ancient Ancestor's son and was stated by members of the Ancient Clan to allow him to create his own Ancient Clan.

• Celestial Bloodline (Celestial Clan)
• Royal: Wang Lin was first given the Celestial Bloodline by Lian Daofei in the world of the Nether Beast. Later, Wang Lin obtains a Celestial Vein formed by the hair of the Celestial Ancestor allowing him to fuse celestial energy and ancient energy to a certain degree.

• Ancestor: Wang Lin later obtains the head of the Celestial Ancestor, which he refines into his Grand Empyrean Sun and is implied to have increased the quality of his bloodline as well.
