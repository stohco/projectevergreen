History [ ] Brilliant Void Star System was the original name but later changed to "Alliance Star System" after the Cultivation Alliance rose to power. The Rain Celestial Realm existed above it in the past. Cultivators [ ] In the Brilliant Void Star System, slaughter is part of the daily life. Those that could survive and reach the 2nd Step or the peak of the 1st Step have all been through a lot. They are all as sly as a fox and extremely cunning. Structure [ ] The Cultivation Alliance is the administrative body of this starsystem. It is split into two sects, four temples, and eight realms. The Peak [ ] In the Alliance Star System , a rank 7 cultivation planet is the peak existence on the surface. Rank 8 cultivation planets are very rare, so much so that the entire Alliance Star System only has three. As for the most mysterious rank 9 cultivation planet, there is only one in the entire Alliance Star System . Rankings [ ] Countries are ranked from 1 to 5, based on the cultivation level of their strongest member Rank 1 country has a Foundation Establishment cultivator at its peak Rank 2 country has a Core Formation cultivator at its peak Rank 3 country has a Nascent Soul cultivator at its peak Rank 4 country has a Soul Formation cultivator at its peak Rank 4 and above countries receive resources from the Alliance in exchange for services and are obliged to respond to a call to arms if given. Rank 5 country has a Soul Transformation cultivator at its peak Rank 6 country has a Ascendent cultivator at its peak. Moreover, the Cultivation Alliance gives the country a cultivation planet along with the Cultivation Planet Crystal to control its people.
