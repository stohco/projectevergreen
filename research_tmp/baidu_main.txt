Void Annihilation level, truly transcended; until descending from the Ninth Bridge, achieving the perfection of the Fourth Step. Through the conflict between soul, divine sense, and Dao charm, one can open up a world within the illusory, forming a cycle of reincarnation within this world. The confrontation between these cycles of reincarnation serves as the primary factor determining everything. Those who leap beyond this Dao realm, forging a new path, are the ones who Tread Heaven from the other shore. Essentially, walking a single Dao to its end and becoming its source is the Fourth Step. Realizing the essence of the Fifth Step until accomplishing it is the cultivation of the Fourth Step. However, not all Daos allow the prover to become the source. For example, fundamental and necessary Daos of the great universe like the Five Elements are not something cultivators can control. When proving the Dao, it may seem to transform into a source, but in reality, it is only one among many, not the only one. The difference between these two points is the distinction between a pseudo-source and a true source. Even if a Dao prover possesses the sources of many Daos, they themselves will have one life-bound Great Dao. This Dao is invariably the foundation of oneself; all cultivation revolves around this life-bound Great Dao. The strength of this Dao's source is the fundamental basis of the Dao prover. I Shall Seal the Heavens Beyond the Transcendence Realm Can easily shatter a great universe, achieve establishing a Dao with a single word, perceive the existence of the Huangtian Star Ring, and can reshape everything except oneself as desired, being referred to as a Paragon. The most fundamental difference between "words becoming law" and "establishing a Dao with a single word" is that the laws gathered by "words becoming law," while seemingly omnipotent, are actually principles that originally existed in the world. In contrast, "establishing a Dao with a single word" is a complete creation from nothing, forcibly added, and once added, it exists eternally.
