## A. Cultivation Arts (foundational methods)

### Underworld Ascension Method / Yellow Springs Ascension Art (黄泉升窍诀)
- **Chapter learned:** Ch. 86 (Donghua S1 EP8)
- **How learned:** Taught by Situ Nan (the remnant soul inside the Heaven Defying Pearl). The Ancient Underworld Ascension Method was a Ji Realm cultivation method; what Situ Nan transmits is a reorganized version with a slim chance of success.
- **Category:** cultivation-art (Demonic / Yin-path)
- **Realm required:** Foundation Establishment to use safely; cultivates Yin Qi toward Core Formation
- **What it does:** Divided into 9 layers. Uses places of extreme Yin as cultivation sites. The cultivator enters a near-death state, absorbs extreme-Yin spiritual power, and forms a **Cold Dan** (cold core) that dramatically boosts the success rate of forming a Golden Core. Also catalyst for reaching the **Ji Realm** (Extreme Realm).
- **Cost/limitations:** Forces a near-death state during cultivation (body is vulnerable). Requires locating extreme-Yin sites (Yin Energy Detection Technique used to find them — Yin quality graded Ordinary / Good / Dense / Extreme). Side-effect: produces cold-attribute **Blue Flames** (Ch. 121).
- **Evolution:** Yields the Cold Dan → Golden Core; cultivates Red Lightning / Ji Realm Divine Sense (Ch. 127). Pairs with Yin Energy Detection Technique. Eventually superseded by Celestial/Ancient Order cultivation but remains the early-game engine of Wang Lin's rise.
- **Canon confidence:** 5

### Vermilion Bird Burning Heaven Art (朱雀焚天功)
- **Chapter learned:** implied early; the Vermilion-Bird-lineage core art Situ Nan transmits alongside the Underworld Ascension Method
- **How learned:** Taught by Situ Nan (2nd-Gen Vermilion Bird lineage)
- **Category:** cultivation-art (Fire-attribute, Vermilion Bird sect-lineage)
- **Realm required:** Qi Condensation → Ascendant
- **What it does:** Foundational Vermilion Bird cultivation art that opens the path to the Vermilion Bird Mark awakenings (1st–4th).
- **Cost/limitations:** Requires the Vermilion Bird Sequence to fully unlock.
- **Evolution:** Feeds into the Vermilion Bird Mark awakenings, Ethereal Fire, and ultimately the Fire Essence / Fire Essence True Body.
- **Canon confidence:** 4 (named in situ_nan_baidu as one of the arts Situ Nan imparted to Wang Lin)

### Nine Cycle Celestial Refining Tactic / Nine Revolutions Refining Immortal Art (九转炼仙诀)
- **Chapter learned:** Ch. 493 (S1 EP106)
- **How learned:** An imitation of a low-quality celestial spell created by the **All-Seer** — Wang Lin acquires it as part of his Celestial Cultivation induction on Planet Tian Yun (under All-Seer / Tianyunzi). Baidu source lists it as Nine Revolutions Refining Immortal Art taught via Tianyunzi's clone.
- **Category:** cultivation-art / celestial-spell (immortal-power cycling)
- **Realm required:** Soul Transformation and up (uses immortal/celestial power)
- **What it does:** Causes immortal power inside the body to slowly rotate in a peculiar spiral shape before release — equivalent to the power of a Golden Immortal. Can produce up to 9 revolutions. The longer the power is held in the body before release, the greater the output.
- **Cost/limitations:** Time-delay tradeoff: more revolutions = more power but longer charge time.
- **Evolution:** Stacks with Rapid Spell Art for fast spinning; foundational to late-game Empyrean Exalt reproduction via Rapid Spell Technique mass production.
- **Canon confidence:** 5

### Ancient God Tactic (古神诀)
- **Chapter learned:** Ch. 190
- **How learned:** Inherited from Tu Si's memory legacy inside the Ancient God's body (the Land of the Ancient God). Essence of the art is one word: **plunder** — plunder everything.
- **Category:** cultivation-art (Ancient Order / body-cultivation)
- **Realm required:** usable from Qi Condensation but true body reconstruction at Ch. 199 (1-Star Ancient God)
- **What it does:** Reconstructs the cultivator's body as an Ancient God. Outer-body cultivation track (the main body sinks into the earth to absorb spiritual energy while the Avatar cultivates normally). Allows absorbing spiritual energy, pills, and inheritance fragments as stars.
- **Cost/limitations:** Forces a hybrid-track existence: main body becomes Ancient God (poor bloodline initially), Avatar becomes the Qi-Cultivator vessel. Hair turns white after absorbing Tu Si's legacy.
- **Evolution:** 1-Star (Ch.199) → 2-Star (Ch.285) → 3-Star (Ch.391) → 4-Star (Ch.708 pseudo / 940 official) → 5-Star (Ch.944) → 6-Star (Ch.1160 pseudo) → 7-Star (Ch.1290 pseudo / 1472 / 1538 golden) → 13-Star Ancient Clan (7G/6D) (Ch.1539) → 20-Star (7G/7M/6D) (Ch.1577) → 24-Star (8G/8M/8D) (Ch.1705) → 26-Star (9G/8M/9D) (Ch.1777) → 27-Star (Ch.2003). Reaches Half-Heaven-Trampling (Ch.2062) and Heaven-Trampling (Ch.2087) via this track.
- **Canon confidence:** 5

- **Chapter learned:** Ch. 192

### Indestructible Immortal Body / Undying Immortal Body (不灭仙体)
- **Chapter learned:** implied ~Ch.1509+ (when Lian Daofei forcibly transmits it; Wang Lin's Dao-Ancient bloodline initially rejects it)
- **How learned:** Transmitted by **Lian Daofei** (madman, brother of Immortal Emperor Lian Daozhen) inside the Nether Beast. Lian Daofei fused his supreme Immortal bloodline into Wang Lin and imparted the art.
- **Category:** cultivation-art (defensive / body-tempering, Immortal-lineage)
- **Realm required:** Mid-to-late 3rd Step
- **What it does:** Forges an undying physical body; later inspires Wang Lin's self-created **Undying Ancient Finger** (Ch.1639) and **Undying Ancient Body** (Ch.1728).
- **Cost/limitations:** Initially rejected by Wang Lin's Dao-Ancient bloodline. After awakening, Lian Daofei imparts numerous divine abilities alongside.
- **Evolution:** Inspirational source for two of Wang Lin's original spells.
- **Canon confidence:** 4

### Rapid Spell Art (速咒术)
- **Chapter learned:** Ch. 1867
- **How learned:** Got after killing **Xu Decai** of the Green Devil Continent
- **Category:** cultivation-art / spell-amplifier
- **Realm required:** 3rd Step (Arcane Void and up)
- **What it does:** Walks an extreme path that lets the caster cast spells at terrifying speed. The key is opening **nine spell veins** in the body, each increasing casting speed greatly. Spell veins are actually a special method that uses sealed time-energy to accelerate casting.
- **Cost/limitations:** Each spell vein requires sealing a portion of cultivation/energy; extreme path means failure during opening is fatal.
- **Evolution:** Becomes the engine of Wang Lin's Void Tribulant breakthrough — combined with mass-produced fused spells, it lets him reproduce the effect of an Empyrean Exalt fusing Dao into their body. Central to his Ch.1907 breakthrough.
- **Canon confidence:** 5

### Dao Fusion (道融 — Dao Spell of Dao Master Blue Dream)
- **Chapter learned:** Ch. 1322
- **How learned:** Taught by / stolen from Dao Master Blue Dream's lineage
- **Category:** cultivation-art / Dao-spell (fusing art)
- **Realm required:** Nirvana Shatterer and up
- **What it does:** Not an attack or defense — a Dao Spell that fuses your spells with your essence in a special way. If learned, lets the user fuse all spells into tens of millions of different ones, or all into one.
- **Cost/limitations:** Dao Spell — comprehension-gated, not raw power.
- **Evolution:** The mechanical basis for Wang Lin's later Essence fusion breakthroughs (combining Fire/Earth/Water etc.).
- **Canon confidence:** 5

### Celestial Slaughter Art (屠仙术)
- **Chapter learned:** Ch. 504 (S1 EP107)
- **How learned:** Celestial Spell Wang Lin obtained from **Grey-robed All-Seer** (the All-Seer) via Tianyunzi's clone. Baidu: learned from Tianyunzi's clone.
- **Category:** combat (life-seal technique, celestial-spell)
- **Realm required:** Soul Transformation+
- **What it does:** Condenses life force from slaughters into **life seals** that protect the user; won't dissipate unless the last life seal is destroyed.
- **Cost/limitations:** Initially incomplete — after gathering exactly 10 million life seals, the seals envelop the user into a true-life seal (this is the trap Bei Lou warned Wang Lin about via the Shi Realm).
- **Evolution:** Wang Lin discovered Tianyunzi's malicious intent, forcibly expelled it inside a crevice in the Demon Spirit Land, and merged the slaughtering origin into his **first Immortal Guard**. Re-cultivated after wounding Tianyunzi at Qinglin's cave dwelling and obtaining the Slaughter Origin.
- **Canon confidence:** 5

### Karma Whip (因果鞭)
- **Chapter learned:** Ch. 731 (Donghua EP147)
- **How learned:** With the help of the **Soul Lasher** treasure, Wang Lin tried to understand his Karma Domain — the Soul Lasher was eventually fused with his Karma Dao to become the Karma Whip. (Per items page: Soul Lasher → fused with Karma Dao = Karma Whip.)

- **Realm required:** Ascendant+ (Karma Domain formed at Ch. 731)
- **What it does:** Manifests the Karma Domain. Per Baidu: directly attacks the primordial spirit. After being burned by Ghostly Sky Fire and nourished by Wang Lin's concept, it became the Cause-Effect Whip. Has a restraining effect on soul-type entities. Once used to cleave open 7 million worlds with a single whip in the Outer Realm.
- **Cost/limitations:** Wields karmic backlash if used against those with no karmic link.
- **Evolution:** Started as Soul Lasher treasure → fused with Karma Domain → Karma Whip. Later one of Wang Lin's hallmark Origin-sword treasures (Karma Sword) — one of the seven Origin Swords.
- **Canon confidence:** 5

### Mountains Crumble / Mountain Collapse (山崩)
- **Chapter learned:** Ch. 1105 (4th of Bai Fan's Six Paths Triple Arts)
- **How learned:** One of the later three Celestial Emperor Bai Fan spells. Baidu: taught by Immortal Lord Qing Shui; one of the Six Paths Triple Techniques.
- **Category:** combat (mountain-summon/collapse, celestial-spell)
- **Realm required:** Nirvana Cleanser+
- **What it does:** Summons illusionary volcanoes, fuses them into a real volcano, and crumbles it. Limit: 2 crumbles per volcano, max 6 mountains (Qing Shui's cap). Baidu: fuses immortal power into spiritual sense; the collapse of the mountain is also the collapse of spiritual sense. Materializes a sky-obscuring volcanic peak out of thin air.
- **Cost/limitations:** Cap of 6 mountains per casting cycle; each volcano only crumbles twice.
- **Evolution:** Mountain Crumble triggers Wang Lin's breakthrough to mid Nirvana Cleanser (Ch. 1102) and lets his Karma Domain reach completion; opens path to True-and-False Domain.
- **Canon confidence:** 5

### The Ji Realm Curse and Resolution

- **Curse origin:** The Ji Realm (Extreme Realm) is the result of Wang Lin's clan annihilation trauma combined with the Underworld Ascension Method's catalyst. Spiritual Energy ascends to Ji Realm and refines into Divine Sense (Ch.127, after FE Early). The Ji Realm is originally a Ji-Realm method of the Ancient Underworld Ascension Method.
- **Conflict:** The Ji Realm interferes with breakthroughs — at Nascent Soul it conflicts with the Avatar's formation. The Avatar workaround breaks the lifespan drawback of the Divine Path technique.
- **Temporary sealing:** At Soul Formation Early (Ch.272), Wang Lin abandons his Ji Realm temporarily.
- **Resolution path:** Ji Realm eventually becomes one of Wang Lin's 9 accompanying thunders — the **Ji Thunder** (Ch.1368, 7th accompanying thunder). The 9 accompanying thunders complete his Thunder Essence and allow him to break through to 3rd Step by stacking Essences instead of enduring Heaven's Blights.

### The Heaven-Defying Bead's Evolution Stages

The Heaven-Defying Pearl (避天珠) is the core plot treasure — Wang Lin's life-defining artifact. Per Baidu: the core of the Realm-Defining Compass; one can truly possess it only when the Five Elements attributes are perfected. Wang Lin's clone Lu Mo borrowed the Realm-Defining Compass from Old Man Miesheng and ultimately blasted it open using the Dream Dao technique. He then sent the Heaven-Defying Pearl through countless tens of thousands of years to the main body Wang Lin. Already fused with Wang Lin's primordial spirit; the Five Elements have gathered; successfully recognized its master.

**Functions:**
- **Spatial folding** — used by Situ Nan to trap enemies in multi-dimensional space.
- **Time acceleration** — 100× time flow inside vs outside world. Wang Lin uses it for closed-door cultivation acceleration throughout the early story.
- **Causality tracing** — Situ Nan uses it to briefly trace back causality (found Tuo Sen's weakness).
- **Heaven-defying fate-altering** — Situ Nan's Green Soul essence resonates with the bead, enabling heaven-defying fate-altering.
- **Dream-space** — where Wang Lin's early Qi Condensation acceleration takes place (10.5 months of real time = 13 years of bead time).
- **Yellow Spring Secret Realm** — Situ Nan opened one inside for Wang Lin's secluded cultivation to break through to Infant Transformation stage.
- **Body-tempering via heavenly tribulation** — Situ Nan used a projection of the bead to trigger heavenly tribulation, converting it into body-tempering energy.
- **Soul-protecting** — protected Wang Lin from the Dao Devil Sect Master's Thunder Essence True Body will-erasure (Ch.1892).
- **Essence cultivation engine** — Ethereal Fire is the first and only of its kind to be Cultivated (rather than fixed) thanks to the bead.
- **Source recovery** — allowed Wang Lin to restore himself after being absorbed by Sovereign's Seven Colored Flower (Ch.1221).
- **Sense-spread** — Ch.1040: with the bead's help, Wang Lin's sense spread across the Alliance Star System.
- **Origin:** originates from the Ancient Divine Realm. The original object consisted of 9 parts, with the Heaven Defying Pearl being its most important part. Root cause of the great war in the Ancient Immortal Domain. Bestowed by the Seven-Colored Immortal Venerable to the Realm-Sealing Supreme as proof of authority over the Inner Realm. Main storyline treasure of Renegade Immortal.

**Cosmology note (cross-novel):** It was once the treasure used by the Dao-Wuya cultivator Xuan Zang as he traversed the Vast Expanse. Su Ming successfully seized it after possession and controlled it. Before Su Ming died, he used it to locate his friend the Bald Feather Crane (i.e., Tian Yunzi), thus arriving in the Inverse Dust Realm — implying the bead exists across the Ergenverse cosmology.
